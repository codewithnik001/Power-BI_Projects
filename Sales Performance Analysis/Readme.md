# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales performance data using Power BI to uncover key insights related to revenue trends, product performance, and sales representative effectiveness.

The goal of this analysis is to identify growth patterns, revenue drivers, and opportunities for improving overall sales performance.

---

## 📁 Dataset Summary
- Total Revenue: ~4.1M  
- Total Transactions: 6,606  
- Total Units Sold: ~463K  
- Sales Representatives: 127  
- Products: 90  
- Vendors: 19  
- Time Period: FY26 Q1 & FY26 Q2  

---

## 📌 Key Features & Analysis

### 🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Overview of revenue, transactions, and units sold  
- Distribution of revenue across products, vendors, and sales reps  
- Weekly revenue trends  

---

### 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Revenue per Sales Rep  
- Average Revenue per Transaction  
- Average Units per Transaction  

These KPIs help evaluate sales productivity and transaction efficiency.

---

### 📈 Time-Based Analysis
- Week-over-Week (WoW) Growth Analysis  
- Quarter-over-Quarter (QoQ) Growth Analysis  

📌 Key Insight:  
Revenue increased by ~15.47% from FY26 Q1 to FY26 Q2.

---

### 🧩 Revenue Share Analysis
- Revenue distribution by Vendor, Product, and Sales Rep  

📌 Key Insight:  
Revenue is highly concentrated among a few key contributors.

---

### 🏆 Top & Bottom Performers
- Top-performing products and sales representatives  
- Lowest-performing products and reps  

📌 Example:  
- Top Product: SAASPCMS01 (~2.1M)  
- Top Rep: AR (~2.18M)  
- Lowest performers contribute minimal revenue  

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Vendor V2 contributes the majority of total revenue (~3.4M)  
- A small number of products and reps drive most of the revenue  
- Sales trends show weekly fluctuations with noticeable peaks  
- There is a significant performance gap between top and bottom performers  

---

## 📌 Business Recommendations
- Focus on high-performing products to maximize revenue  
- Improve performance of low-performing sales reps through training  
- Diversify vendor dependency to reduce risk  
- Use trend analysis to optimize sales strategies and campaigns  

---

## ⚙️ Dashboard Refresh & Performance
To ensure scalability and efficiency:

- Scheduled refresh can be configured in Power BI Service  
- Power BI Gateway can be used for on-premise data sources  
- Data model optimized using measures and efficient design  
- Recommended use of incremental refresh for large datasets
